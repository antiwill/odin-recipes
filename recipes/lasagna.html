Description

This is a delicious traditional american-italian neo-Genoese inspired Lasagna recipe.
This will be sure to make everyone happy at family get togethers.

  • 200g pasta sheets, dry
  • 400g tomato, canned, whole
  • 2 onions, finely cut
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 50g tomato puree
